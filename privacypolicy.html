Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use our Reminder & Note app.

Information Collection and Use

Reminder & Note is designed to respect your privacy. All data created within the app (including reminders, notes, and settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Reminders and their settings
  • Notes associated with reminders
  • Custom images used in reminders
  • App preferences and settings
  • Reminder history

This data remains on your device and is only accessible through the app. It can be deleted by removing the app from your device.

Device Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send reminder alerts at your specified times
  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to customize reminders with your own images

In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases for premium features. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's App Store, and we do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Remove All Data" option in the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All data storage is local to the device and under the control of the device owner.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app accordingly.
